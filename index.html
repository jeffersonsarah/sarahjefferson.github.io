About

HelpHub is a place that unites passionate volunteers with local charities who would like to donate their time. This is a place to go through a list of charities and see which one most aligns with your values, beliefs, and your mission. You are able to read a little bit about each charity and figure out which one you would most likley want to be assosiated with.

Our Mission

Out mission here at HelpHub is to get you connected to your community. It can be a local community or a community you feel drawn to that as across the country. We want you to feel safe a nd connected.
